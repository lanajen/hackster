Welcome!

What?

We built a robotic ukulele ! This project was born during the amazing Science Hack Day.

We explored the propagation of sound waves through a gaseous medium, when originating from a soprano stringed instrument as activated by an electronic agent.

A demo video is available here but we're in boing boing and NPR too!

More info on the artist blog page.

How?

  • 4 of the smallest servomotors from our best last time minute friend, "Shadio Rack" ;p
  • The Arduino clone called Teensy using the Servo library.

Who?

  • The world famous hacker called Merlin AKA Alex (@glowascii).
  • Myself, interhactively yours, Drix.
